What We DON'T Do

  • No Data Storage: We do not store your assessment responses, answers, or personal information in any database.
  • No User Profiles: We do not create, maintain, or store user accounts or profiles.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your behavior across sessions or websites.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: We have no affiliation with data gathering organizations and never sell or share your data.
  • No Cookies for Tracking: We only use essential cookies required for the assessment to function during your session.

How Your Assessment Works

All assessment processing happens in real-time in your browser session. Your responses are analyzed immediately to generate your report, and then they're gone. We don't send your answers to a server for storage.
